Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 7, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Finify ("we", "us", "our"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

Finify is designed with a privacy-first approach. We minimize data collection and never store raw document content.

Information you provide:

  • Subscription details you manually enter (name, amount, billing cycle)
  • Account preferences and settings
  • Google account connection for cloud sync (securely stored)

Information we derive from bank statements:

  • Merchant name, subscription amount, and billing cycle
  • Confidence scores for detected subscriptions

Information we never collect or store:

  • Raw bank statement documents or their full content
  • Full account numbers or personal identifiers
  • Names, phone numbers, or physical addresses
  • Order IDs or transaction details beyond subscription amounts

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To display and manage your subscription tracking
  • To send billing reminders (with your permission)
  • To provide spending insights and budget tracking
  • To sync data across devices (Premium feature, optional)

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is primarily stored locally on your device. If you enable cloud sync (Premium feature), data is encrypted and stored securely using Supabase with row-level security. OAuth tokens are stored using platform-secure storage (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore). We never store secrets in our codebase.

5. Bank Statement Analysis

If you upload a bank statement for scanning, the document is processed ephemerally in a serverless function and immediately discarded. We only store the extracted subscription records, never the raw document data. No account tokens or email content is accessed.

6. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob (non-Premium users): Displays ads, subject to Google's privacy policy
  • RevenueCat: Manages subscriptions, no personal data shared
  • Supabase: Cloud database (Premium sync only), encrypted at rest

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all data we store about you
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your data in CSV or PDF format
  • Opt out of notifications

8. Data Deletion

You can delete all your data through Settings → Delete Account. This permanently removes all data from your device, our servers, caches, and job queues. This action cannot be undone.

9. Children's Privacy

Finify is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
