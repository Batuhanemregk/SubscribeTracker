How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Premium subscriptions are billed through the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android). Manage or cancel them in your device's subscription settings: on iOS, Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions; on Android, Play Store → Profile → Payments & subscriptions. Cancel at least 24 hours before the renewal date to avoid the next charge.

How do I restore a purchase?

Open the app, go to the Premium / upgrade screen, and tap Restore Purchases. Your active subscription will be re-applied to your account.

How do I delete my account and data?

In the app, go to Settings → Delete Account. This permanently removes your data from your device, our servers, caches, and job queues. This action cannot be undone.

How is my financial data handled?

Finify never stores the raw content of bank statements. Documents are processed ephemerally and discarded; only derived subscription records are kept. See our Privacy Policy for details.
