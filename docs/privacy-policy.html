Privacy Policy

Effective March 2026 · Last updated March 10, 2026

Finify is a subscription tracking mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Email Address: Collected when you sign up via Google OAuth (Pro users only)
  • Subscription Data: Names, amounts, billing dates, and categories of subscriptions you manually enter
  • Uploaded Documents: Bank statements or screenshots you upload for AI-powered subscription detection. These are processed in real-time and not stored on our servers.

Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and app version for crash reporting
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous app interaction data to improve the app experience
  • Advertising ID: Used by Google AdMob to serve ads (Standard tier only)

We Do NOT Collect

  • Bank account credentials or login information
  • Full bank account numbers
  • Social Security numbers or government IDs
  • Location data
  • Contact lists or phone data

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide the Service: Store and display your subscription data, calculate spending analytics, and send billing reminders
  • AI Processing: Process uploaded documents to detect subscriptions via the OpenAI API. Document content is not stored after extraction.
  • Cloud Sync: Sync subscription data across your devices (Pro users only, via Supabase)
  • Notifications: Send local push notifications for upcoming payments and free trial expirations
  • Advertising: Display relevant ads via Google AdMob (Standard tier only)
  • Improve the App: Analyze anonymous usage patterns to enhance features and fix bugs

3. Data Storage & Security

  • Local-First: All subscription data is stored locally on your device by default using encrypted storage
  • Cloud Sync (Pro): If enabled, data is synced to Supabase servers (EU-Central-1, Frankfurt) with encryption in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Row-Level Security: Cloud-synced data is protected by database-level security policies ensuring you can only access your own data
  • Secure Credentials: Authentication tokens are stored in your device's secure keychain (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore)

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate Finify:

Service Purpose Data Shared
Supabase Auth, cloud sync, edge functions Email, subscription data (Pro)
RevenueCat In-app purchases Anonymous user ID, purchase history
Google AdMob Advertising (Standard) Advertising ID, device info
OpenAI AI subscription detection Document text (processed, not stored)
Apple/Google Distribution, payments Standard platform data

5. Data Retention

  • Local Data: Stored on your device until you delete the app or clear app data
  • Cloud Data (Pro): Retained while your account is active. Deleted within 30 days of account deletion request.
  • Uploaded Documents: Processed in real-time and discarded immediately. No bank statements or screenshots are stored on our servers.
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage data is retained for up to 12 months

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data we store about you (available in-app via Settings > Data Export)
  • Export: Download your subscription data as CSV or PDF (Pro feature)
  • Delete: Delete your account and all associated cloud data by contacting us
  • Opt Out of Ads: Upgrade to Pro for an ad-free experience, or adjust ad preferences in your device settings
  • Withdraw Consent: Disable cloud sync at any time in Settings

For users in the EU (GDPR) and California (CCPA), additional rights apply:

  • Right to data portability
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to be forgotten

7. Children's Privacy

Finify is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
